GlassBox AI — Animal Symbols

Trust · Transparency · Multi-Agent Negotiation · From Nature, Fables & Folklore

01
Glass Frog
Hyalinobatrachium · Costa Rica
A frog with a transparent belly. You can see its heart beating, organs working. It hides NOTHING.
"The animal that IS a glass box"
02
The Waggle Vote
Honeybee · Real Science
When bees need a new hive, scouts dance to argue locations. The swarm literally VOTES through dance. Democracy in nature.
"Multi-agent debate is 80M years old"
03
Owl Council
Panchatantra · Crows & Owls
In the Panchatantra, owls hold night councils to debate strategy. Three wise owls — one sees, one judges, one decides.
"Wisdom is a council, not a king"
04
Glasswing
Greta oto · Central America
A butterfly with completely transparent wings. Predators can see right through it — and it STILL survives. Transparency as strength.
"Nothing to hide, nothing to fear"
05
Wolf Pack
Canis lupus · Worldwide
Wolves hunt as a coordinated team. Alpha leads, beta flanks, scout reports. Absolute trust — one wrong move and the hunt fails.
"Trust your pack or die alone"
06
Elephant Council
Loxodonta · Africa
Elephant herds make decisions by council. The matriarch consults elders, weighs experience, and the herd follows. They never forget a lesson.
"Trust is built on memory"
07
Mice & Elephants
Panchatantra · Mitra-labha
Tiny mice freed trapped elephants by gnawing their ropes. The elephants later saved the mice from a flood. Small agents + big agents = mutual trust.
"Every agent matters, regardless of size"
08
Dolphin Pod
Tursiops · Worldwide Oceans
Dolphins use echolocation — sound waves that reveal everything hidden. They share the map with the entire pod. Total information transparency.
"Ping the truth, share with everyone"
09
Lion & Rabbit
Panchatantra · Kakolukiyam
A rabbit showed a lion his own reflection in a well. The lion attacked his reflection and fell in. Truth (transparency) defeated brute force.
"The mirror doesn't lie"
10
Crane Formation
Grus · Worldwide Migration
Cranes fly in V-formation. The leader rotates — when one tires, another takes over. Trust that the next leader will also find the way.
"Rotate leadership, share the burden"
